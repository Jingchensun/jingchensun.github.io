About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in computer science from the State University of New York at Buffalo. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Changyou Chen. Before that, I obtained my Master's degree at Zhejiang University and my Bachelor's degree at North China Electric Power University .

My research interests mainly focus on computer vision and deep Learning. Currently, I am working on the efficient transfer learning of large Vision-Language (VL) models. My research goal to is make the large VL models achieve efficient open-vocabulary image recognization in downstream tasks.
