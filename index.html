About me

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at State University of New York at Buffalo jointly supervised by Prof. Lu Su and Prof. Aidong Zhang. Before that, I received my B.S. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015.

My research interests are broadly in Sensing Systems, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Data Mining. I'm particularly interested in the areas of Human Sensing and Human Pose Estimation using wireless devices.
