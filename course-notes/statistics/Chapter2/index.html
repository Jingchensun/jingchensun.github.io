Chapter 2: Summary Statistics

Section 2.1: Basic Statistics

Given data: \( \{8, 3, 14, 1, 5, 7, 21, 4, 10, 3\} \)

Ordered: \( \{1, 3, 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 10, 14, 21\} \)

  • \( n = 10 \)
  • \( \bar{x} = 7.6 \)
  • Median = 6
  • Min = 1, Max = 21

Section 2.2: Standard Deviation

\( s = \sqrt{ \frac{1}{n-1} \sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2 } = 6.077 \)

For population: \( s_{pop} = \sqrt{ \frac{1}{n} \sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2 } \)

Section 2.3: Quartiles and Box Plot

  • Q1 = 3.25
  • Median = 6
  • Q3 = 9.5
  • Five-number summary = (1, 3.25, 6, 9.5, 21)

\( IQR = Q3 - Q1 = 6.25 \)

Upper fence: \( Q3 + 1.5 \cdot IQR = 18.875 \)

Lower fence: \( Q1 - 1.5 \cdot IQR = -6.125 \)
